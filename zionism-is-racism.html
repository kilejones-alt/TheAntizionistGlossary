UN slogan / racism charge / legitimacy attack

Zionism Is Racism

The phrase revives the Cold War charge made famous by UN Resolution 3379 and treats Jewish national life as racism by definition.

This placeholder will trace how “Zionism is racism” moved from Soviet and Arab-bloc diplomacy into campus speech, protest signs, and boycott campaigns. The entry should show how the phrase avoids Israel’s actual laws, wars, minorities, and security record, and instead declares Jewish national life guilty from the start.
