Intifada

The word carries the history of Palestinian uprisings, including suicide bombings, shootings, stabbings, and attacks on Israeli civilians.

This placeholder will explain how “intifada” is used in protest settings as a word of revolt while softening the history of the First and Second Intifadas. The entry should make clear that many Jews hear the term through the memory of buses, cafés, hotels, and streets targeted during those years.
