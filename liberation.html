Liberation

The word can mean Palestinian freedom, but in antizionist use it can also mean a land emptied of Israel.

This placeholder will separate ordinary calls for Palestinian dignity from uses of “liberation” that leave no place for Israel or Jews as a people with national rights. The entry should ask what the word permits, what it rejects, and whether Israeli Jews survive inside the promised future.
