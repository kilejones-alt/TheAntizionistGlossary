Author

Kile Jones researches and writes on modern antizionism, history, and the uses of language. He holds two master’s degrees in Religious Studies from Boston University and writes at The Inverted World. His work has appeared in Philosophy Now, The Humanist, The Routledge Guide, and Free Inquiry.
