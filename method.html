The Antizionist Glossary

Method

This page explains how the glossary selects terms, separates description from criticism, and uses primary or close-to-primary sources wherever possible.

What this glossary does

It maps modern antizionist vocabulary: how terms are used, what they imply, where they come from, and how they function.

What this glossary does not do

It does not treat every criticism of Israeli policy as antisemitic. It distinguishes criticism from delegitimizing language, libel, inversion, and exclusionary usage.

Evidence standards

Use primary documents, direct organizational statements, treaty texts, legal definitions, institutional records, and dated examples wherever possible.
