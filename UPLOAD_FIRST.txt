UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS

1. Open this zip.
2. Press Ctrl + A.
3. Press Ctrl + C.
4. Make a Desktop folder called upload.
5. Open the upload folder.
6. Press Ctrl + V.
7. GitHub repo -> Add file -> Upload files.
8. Click choose your files.
9. Open the upload folder.
10. Press Ctrl + A.
11. Click Open.
12. Click Commit changes.
13. Wait 1-3 minutes.
14. Refresh the site with Ctrl + F5.

Do not upload the zip itself. Upload the files inside it.
