EASY UPLOAD VERSION - NO FOLDERS

1. Unzip this zip.
2. Open the unzipped folder.
3. Press Ctrl + A to select every file.
4. In GitHub, click Add file > Upload files.
5. Click choose your files.
6. Select every file from this folder.
7. Click Open.
8. Click Commit changes.
9. Wait 1-3 minutes.
10. Refresh the website with Ctrl + F5.

Do not upload the zip itself.
